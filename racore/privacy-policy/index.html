Racore — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

Racore is a racket-sports scoring app for Apple Watch and iOS. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to be transparent about what data the app handles.

Data Collection

Racore does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All match scores and history are stored locally on your devices and synced only between your paired Apple Watch and iPhone using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework.

Analytics & Tracking

Racore does not include any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. We do not track your location, usage patterns, or any other behavioral data.

Third-Party Services

Racore does not integrate with any third-party services that would have access to your data.

Data Storage

All data created within Racore (match scores, player names, match history) remains on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

Children's Privacy

Racore does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not require account creation or personal information of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
